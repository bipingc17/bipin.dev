01 / about

a brief self-description.

I'm Bipin — an engineer drawn to the messy, hands-on intersection of robotics and software. Most of my time goes into ROS, Python, and the occasional Ubuntu rabbit hole, learning by shipping things that actually work (and, more often, debugging why they don't).

I've been working with multi-robot swarms, custom message generation, launch files that refuse to behave, and the kind of incremental wins that make engineering feel worth it. On the side, I'm building out a deeper Python toolkit — data analysis, automation, web — because tools compound.

I'm looking for a role where I can build, break, and learn fast alongside people who care about the craft.
